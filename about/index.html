About

Decision Design Lab sits at the intersection of data, design and decision making. It's a space for exploring how information can be shaped into clarity — through visualization, interaction design, and thoughtful structure.

What I Do

I work with data and design to build tools and experiences that support better decisions. This means transforming complex information into clear, interactive artifacts — dashboards, visual explorations, and lightweight applications that make patterns visible and choices easier.

My practice spans data visualization, product design, and front-end development. Each project is an opportunity to find the right balance between analytical rigor and intuitive communication.

How I Work

I believe in working in the open. This site is a living portfolio — part showcase, part sandbox. Projects here range from polished pieces to rough experiments, because the process matters as much as the output.

My approach is iterative and grounded in real constraints. I favor simple tools, honest materials, and designs that respect the viewer's intelligence. Every element should earn its place.

If you're interested in collaborating or just want to talk about data, design, or decision-making, get in touch.
